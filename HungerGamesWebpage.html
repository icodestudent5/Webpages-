The Hunger Games is a book that every Middle Schooler needs to read. The book was written by Suzanne Collins. It takes place in the future after the world was destroyed. North America was the only place left. There are twelve districts that were created from the ruins and was later named Panem. The Capitol made the Hunger Games to pay tribute to those who died. One girl and boy, from the age of twelve to eighteen, are chosen to take part in the Games. A total of twenty four children compete and to win they must fight until only one child survives. The main character , Katniss Everdeen, volunteers in place of her younger sister. Will she be able to survive along with her new found friends? Read to find out!
